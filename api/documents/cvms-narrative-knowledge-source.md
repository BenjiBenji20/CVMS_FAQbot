# Colour Variant Multimedia Services

**Tagline:** Visuals that Speak. Events that Connect.

## Company Overview

**Company Name:** Colour Variant Multimedia Services  
(formerly Colour Variant Photo and Video Services)

**Business Type:** Full-service multimedia and creative production company

**Location:** Caloocan City, National Capital Region (NCR), Philippines

**Business Registration:** DTI & BIR Registered

## Company Background & History

- Founded on **October 18, 2021** as Colour Variant Photo and Video Services  
- Officially rebranded and expanded on **April 24, 2024** to Colour Variant Multimedia Services

**Original Focus:**  
Exclusively photo and video event coverage

**Mission:**  
To deliver exceptional and unforgettable experiences through creativity, collaboration, and high-quality multimedia solutions.

## Core Services

### Photo & Video Production

Professional services including:

- Event photo and video coverage  
- Creative direction and concept planning  
- On-the-day production  
- Full-service production management  
- Segment flow creation  
- Post-production editing (color grading, same-day edits, cinematic highlights)

#### Types of Events We Cover

- Weddings (full photo + video)  
- Debut / 18th birthday celebrations  
- Corporate events and brand documentation  
- Prenuptial (prenup) & save-the-date shoots  
- Product photography & commercial shoots  
- EXDEAL / brand collaborations & endorsements

### Event Production & Coordination

End-to-end planning and execution:

- Full event planning & creative direction  
- On-the-day and full-service event coordination  
- Scriptwriting & segment flow design  
- Host and program emcee management  
- Sound system, lighting, and LED wall rental  
- Live feed / streaming setup

### Venue & Room Buildouts

Custom permanent or semi-permanent installations:

- Acoustic rooms and music studios  
- Entertainment / home theater room setups  
- LED wall installations (temporary or fixed)

## Client Retainer Packages

Priority booking and special rates with flexible terms:

- 3 months  
- 6 months  
- 1 year  

Ideal for frequent clients, events companies, or brands needing consistent coverage.
